Our Story

Founded by a Doctor
Who Never Stopped
Loving to Teach

Elevate Tutor was born out of a genuine passion for teaching. Our founder — a qualified doctor — spent the entirety of medical school tutoring students in Maths and Science, and discovered something unexpected: the classroom was just as fulfilling as the clinic.

That experience also revealed something important. Students aspiring to study Medicine were rarely getting the calibre of admissions support they needed — guidance from someone who had actually navigated the UCAT, written a successful personal statement, and sat the medical school interviews themselves. So Elevate Tutor was created to fill that gap, while delivering the same expert support across Maths, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics from KS3 to A-Level.

Based in Solihull, we work with students across the West Midlands and beyond — both in-person and online. Every session is shaped by the same ethos: no shortcuts, no generic worksheets, just expert support tailored to your child.
